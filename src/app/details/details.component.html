🗺️ Exploring Molecules in the Pathway

When a pathway is displayed in the panel above, it shows detailed information about all the molecules involved. If an item in the diagram is selected, the corresponding molecules are marked with a 📌 pin icon. Molecules are categorized into subtypes: Protein, Chemical Compound, Sequences (DNA/RNA), and Other.

To download molecule lists: Go to the Download menu on the right side of the Table of Contents (TOC). An expansion panel will appear. Select the desired fields and molecule types, then click Download.

Tissue expression overview
The tissue expression overview displays baseline expression levels of pathway-related proteins across a range of healthy human tissues, based on data from the Human Protein Atlas (HPA).
This view helps you interpret your results by showing where the proteins involved in enriched or regulated pathways are normally expressed. It answers the question: "In which tissues is this pathway typically active under normal conditions?", offering valuable biological context to support your analysis.

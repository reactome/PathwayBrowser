Species comparison analysis
Species comparison analysis helps identify conserved pathway patterns across species. It answers the question: "Is the pathway activity or enrichment I observe in my species also present in another species?"
You can upload data from multiple species (e.g. human, mouse, zebrafish), and Reactome will automatically map their genes or proteins to human equivalents. This allows for cross-species comparison of enriched or regulated pathways, helping you uncover shared biological mechanisms or species-specific differences.

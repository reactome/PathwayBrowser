Species comparison analysis
Species comparison analysis helps identify conserved pathway patterns across species. It answers the question: "Does this human pathway have an equivalent within my species of interest, based on their orthologous genes?"
You can select one species bellow, and Reactome will compare all its orthologous proteins and genes it knows to the human pathways. Ultimately, this is a qualitative enrichment analysis on the proteome of a species, projected onto human pathways.
